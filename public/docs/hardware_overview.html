HOW IT ALL CONNECTS The Three-Device Architecture

Your Windows PC is where you work day-to-day. The Mac Mini lives on your desk and runs 24/7 — it's the always-on infrastructure that handles scheduled agents and the messaging gateway. Your iPhone is how you reach both from anywhere.
